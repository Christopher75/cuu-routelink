About CUU RouteLink

Uganda's Smart Bus Transit Booking Platform — Built for the Future of Public Transport

Get Started Free Contact Us

Our Mission

CUU RouteLink was built to solve a real problem — the lack of a reliable, digital bus booking system in Uganda. Travellers can now search, compare, book, and receive verified digital tickets in minutes, without queuing at a bus park.

What the System Can Do

Secure Authentication

Email-based login with PBKDF2 password hashing, session management, and self-service password reset via email.

Visual Seat Selection

Interactive seat grid showing live availability. Passengers choose exactly where they sit before paying.

QR Code Tickets

Every confirmed booking generates a unique QR code ticket with all passenger and journey details for easy boarding verification.

Interactive Route Maps

OpenStreetMap-powered route visualisation showing departure and arrival locations across Uganda's major cities.

Ratings & Reviews

Passengers can rate and review trips after travel, giving future travellers real social proof to choose the best service.

Admin Analytics

Real-time dashboard with revenue charts, occupancy rates, cancellation stats, top routes, and recent reviews — all in one place.

Email Notifications

Automatic booking confirmation and cancellation emails sent to passengers with full trip details and booking reference.

Live Countdown Timers

My Tickets shows a real-time countdown to departure for every upcoming booking — so passengers never miss their bus.

Print-Ready Tickets

Passengers can print individual tickets or all bookings in a clean, print-optimised layout that hides navigation and shows QR codes.
